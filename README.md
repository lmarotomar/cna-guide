# CNA / HHA Interview Prep Guide
### Bilingual Interview Preparation · Preparación Bilingüe para Entrevista

A comprehensive, bilingual (English / Spanish) study guide designed to help CNA and HHA candidates prepare for job interviews and daily work in healthcare settings.

---

## 🌐 Live Site
👉 **[Open the Guide](https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/cna-guide)**

## 📋 Contents

| Section | Details |
|---------|---------|
| 🎤 Interview Q&A | 40 questions with model answers — General, Situational, Ethics, Teamwork, Technical |
| 📖 Terminology | 50 essential terms with English & Spanish pronunciation guide and audio |
| 💬 Useful Phrases | 75 everyday phrases across 12 categories with phonetic pronunciation |
| 🚨 Emergency Vocabulary | 15 emergency terms with response protocols |
| 💡 Interview Tips | 8 professional tips for interview day |

---

## ✨ Features

- **Fully bilingual** — English and Spanish throughout
- **Audio playback** — Web Speech API, works in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari
- **Phonetic pronunciation** — Every phrase and term includes a Spanish-friendly phonetic guide
- **Study progress tracker** — Tracks which Q&A cards have been reviewed
- **Mobile-friendly** — Works on any smartphone browser
- **100% offline** — No internet required after the page loads
- **No dependencies** — Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no installs

---

## 📱 How to Use

1. Open the link on any smartphone or desktop browser
2. Navigate using the tabs at the top
3. Tap any question to expand the answer
4. Press 🔊 to hear the English pronunciation
5. Use the filter buttons in Terminology to browse by category

---

## 🛠️ Built With

- Pure HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
- Web Speech API for audio
- No external libraries or CDN dependencies

---

## 👩‍⚕️ Intended For

Healthcare students and candidates preparing for CNA (Certified Nursing Assistant) or HHA (Home Health Aide) positions in the United States. Especially designed for bilingual Spanish-English speakers entering the healthcare workforce.
